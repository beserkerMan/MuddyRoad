Dell Laptop (Probably Fine)
£45
Right so this laptop. Bought it in 2019 off a bloke at a car boot. He said it was his work laptop but I don't know what work he was doing on it.

It runs Windows 10 fine. Windows 11 it started crying. Boots up in about 2 minutes which I think is reasonable for a machine that's been through what this machine has been through.

Fan makes a sort of whirring noise when it's thinking hard. I find it comforting. Like it's trying its best.

Battery holds about 45 minutes off charger. Don't let it die or it gets moody. The 'G' key is sticky — was a Ribena incident, I'd rather not discuss it.

Selling because I got a new one. This one deserves a loving home. Or at least a home. Or a shed with WiFi.
